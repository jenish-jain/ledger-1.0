About Us

Ledger is an online tool to keep track of your company's transactions and display it beautifully.
It’s your money, right? The financial core of your business is in good hands with Ledger. Integration is standard, and all information feeds back to General Ledger for pain-free, real-time financial reporting. Get the pulse of your cash flow when it’s convenient for you, not at the end of some arbitrary period of time. With Accounting Software, you can be more efficient and more productive, regardless of the size and scope of your channel.

Features

Data Privacy

We maintain user sessions, so neither are you able to see anyone else's data nor is anyone else able to see yours. Thus your critical informations stay secured with ledger.

Multiple Project creation

You can create multiple projects and work on them individually, with seamless interaction provided in our dashboard.

Quick Analysis

We automatically calculate your profit percentage, cash in hand and other quick measures to have a glance at your finances.

Filter View

Export to PDF
